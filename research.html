Research & Methodology

Data-driven approach to global air quality monitoring

Problem Statement

Air pollution affects billions globally, yet real-time monitoring remains inaccessible to many. Traditional stations are sparse, and satellite data requires expertise to interpret.

AirLens bridges this gap by combining three data sources: ground stations, camera-based AI prediction, and satellite imagery.
