About AirLens

Making air quality data accessible to everyone

Our Mission

AirLens was created to democratize access to air quality information. We believe everyone deserves to know the air they breathe, regardless of their location or technical expertise.

By combining cutting-edge AI, real-time data from 30,000+ monitoring stations, and beautiful 3D visualization, we're making environmental data both accessible and actionable.

Why AirLens?

Air pollution is responsible for 7 million premature deaths annually. Yet most people lack access to reliable, real-time air quality data.

AirLens fills this gap by providing:

  • Real-time global coverage through 30,000+ stations
  • AI-powered prediction from smartphone cameras
  • Beautiful, intuitive 3D visualization
  • Policy research and recommendations
  • 100% free access for everyone

Technology & Accessibility

AirLens is built with modern web technologies and runs entirely in your browser. No installation required, no data collection, complete privacy.

The platform is designed with accessibility in mind, supporting:

  • Keyboard navigation
  • Screen readers
  • High contrast mode
  • Responsive design for all devices
